## What do I do?

I specialize in the use of UX and prototyping tools, with **Figma** as my primary platform. I have extensive knowledge of Design Tokens and **Atomic Design**, consistently helping design teams advance their UI best practices and componentization, all supported by **agile methodologies**.

My experience spans from **component architecture** and Figma engineering to integrating those elements into development, using a unified language across multiple platforms. This skill facilitates refinement between design and development phases. Throughout my development journey, I worked as a Front-End Developer, focusing especially on CSS and component creation across various frameworks.

In Accessibility, I supported the creation of **theming and styling** for color-blind users, high-contrast themes, and dyslexia, as well as assisting in development to enable applications to be navigable via **screen readers**. All of this was always tested with users.

Today, I work as Accessibility Team Lead at Vivo, responsible for team management, planning, and backlog organization.
